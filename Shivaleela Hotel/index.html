Our Story

About Shivaleela Hotel

80 years of passion, tradition, and authentic Indian cuisine crafted with love.

Our Heritage

A Legacy of Taste & Tradition

Shivaleela Hotel, established in 1945 inJogeshwari East, Mumbai, is a well-known North Indian restaurant ownedby Ramdas Patkar. The in-charge isSanath Shetty (8104699941).

The restaurant is recognized for itscommitment to customer satisfactionand quality service, drawing a largecustomer base. Conveniently locatednear Jogeshwari Railway Station, itoffers a variety of North Indian,Chinese, and Tandoori dishes at bothaffordable and moderate prices.

along with home delivery services. Thebusiness plans to expand its offeringsin the future.Every recipe in our kitchen traces its roots to ancient Indian culinary traditions — passed down through generations, refined by our master chefs, and brought to your table with love.
